# Pleased to meet you! We're Sego Media.

### A US-based, full-service event consulting company and rental house.

- We're currently working on **leveraging open-source to streamline core operations, while giving back to the community with upstream contributions.**
    - Resource Planning, Customer Management, Inventory, Backend Virtualization, Customer Operations and more: Over 70% of our operations stack relies on open-source tools written by the community.
    - **Open Source keeps us going**: We believe investing in our employees and the community has outsize impact over simply paying vendors for a gatekept product at stratospheric pricing. Supporting open-source software helps us invest more in our employees, drive faster rollout of new features, and help everyone benefit with more fully-featured systems that can deliver real impact at any scale.

- 📫 Write to us at **hello@segomedia.com**
